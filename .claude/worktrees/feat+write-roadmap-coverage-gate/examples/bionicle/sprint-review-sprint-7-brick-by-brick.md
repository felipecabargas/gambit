# Sprint Review: Sprint 7 — "Brick by Brick"
**Team:** Bionicle Digital (LEGO Group, Copenhagen) · **Dates:** April 28 – May 9, 2025 · **Generated:** 2026-05-07

---

## Executive Summary
The team shipped the AR Mask of Power scanner and a redesigned onboarding flow, pushing D7 retention from 33% to 41% and lifting onboarding completion from 54% to 78% — well past the 70% target. A P1 crash affecting iPhone 12 and older devices hit the scanner launch mid-sprint and was resolved the same week. Multiplayer co-build carries over; all other commitments delivered.

---

## Sprint Goal & Outcome
**Goal:** Ship the AR scanner and onboarding v2 to achieve 70%+ onboarding completion.
**Result:** Partially hit — onboarding target exceeded (78%), scanner launched successfully but triggered a P1 crash on older devices that required an unplanned fix mid-sprint.

---

## Highlights & Wins

- **AR Mask of Power scanner** — 62% of active users scanned at least one physical mask within the first week of launch; physical-to-digital engagement exceeds pre-launch projections.
- **Onboarding v2** — completion rate jumped from 54% to 78% after cutting 5 steps and adding a skip option; drops at step 3 (the previous cliff) are gone.
- **D7 retention up 8 points** — 41% of new installs still active at day 7, up from 33% last sprint; strongest retention figure since soft launch.
- **P1 crash resolved mid-sprint** — AR scanner was crashing on iPhone 12 and older at launch; patched and redeployed within 3 days with no App Store rating impact (still 4.6, 143 ratings).
- **Leaderboard for weekly build challenges** — shipped alongside the push notification system; creates the competitive loop needed to support weekly active user targets.
