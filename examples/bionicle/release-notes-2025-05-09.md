# Release Notes — May 9, 2025

**Released:** May 9, 2025
**Product:** Bionicle Reborn (iOS & Android)

---

## Highlights

The Masks of Power are live. Scan any physical mask with your camera and its power unlocks inside the app — this is the bridge between your shelf and your game that we've been building toward. Alongside the scanner, you can now customise and save your Toa builds, explore the Mata Nui world map, and compete on weekly challenge leaderboards. The Bionicle Reborn universe just got a lot bigger.

---

## New

- **Mask of Power AR scanner:** Point your camera at any physical Bionicle mask to scan it and unlock its power in the app. The more masks you find, the more abilities you unlock.
- **Toa character builder:** Mix and match Toa parts to create your ideal build and save up to 5 loadouts. Swap between them any time.
- **Mata Nui world map:** The island is yours to explore. Tap regions on the world map to uncover lore, hidden story fragments, and mini-games.
- **Daily challenges and story drip:** Opt in to push notifications and get a new challenge or story update every day. The saga continues whether you open the app or not.
- **Weekly build challenge leaderboard:** Your builds now compete. See how your Toa stacks up against the community each week.

## Improved

- **Faster onboarding:** Getting started now takes 4 steps instead of 9. There's also a skip option if you want to jump straight in.

## Fixed

- **AR scanner crash on iPhone 12 and older:** The scanner was crashing on launch for users on iPhone 12 and older models. This is resolved — scanning works across all supported devices.
